What is Ligicate Money Manager

Ligicate Money Manager, is how lay away custodians are independently verified and issued with their ligatures. Ligatures are exchanged with Asset Owners. They provide an asset in custody with a blockchain based independently verifiable representation. In short the Ligicate Money Manager conducts a mini ICO with each custodian by creating and issuing their Ligatures on the Algorand Blockchain.
